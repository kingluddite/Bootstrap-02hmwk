Ann Steffler

I have been raising South African Boerboels for over 20 years. The boerboel is a a large breed dog that requires a carefully crafted diet that supports their nutritional needs during their development without sacrificing the long term stability of their joints and connective tissue.

The result is a very healthy, long livded,large breed dog.
